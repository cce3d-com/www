Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my state information?

Go to Settings in the app and select your state. You can then customize your governor, senators, and representative information for personalized questions.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, the app works completely offline. All questions, explanations, and audio are stored locally on your device.

Are the questions up to date?

Absolutely. Our app is updated regularly with the latest USCIS civics questions and current political information for 2025.

How do I turn on/off audio?

You can control audio settings in the Settings menu. Each question also has a speaker button for individual audio control.

Can I track my progress?

Yes, the app tracks your practice test scores and identifies areas where you need more study.

Is this app affiliated with USCIS?

No, this is an independent study app. However, all content is based on official USCIS materials and guidelines.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't open

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest iOS version
  • Try deleting and reinstalling the app

Audio not working

  • Check your device's volume and mute switch
  • Ensure audio is enabled in the app settings
  • Try using headphones to test audio output
  • Check if other apps can play audio normally

Questions not loading

  • The app works offline, so no internet connection is needed
  • Try restarting the app
  • If problems persist, reinstall the app

Study Tips

Maximize your success with these study strategies:

  • Study regularly: Spend 15-20 minutes daily rather than cramming
  • Use flashcards: Great for memorizing facts and dates

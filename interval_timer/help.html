What is Interval Timer?

Interval Timer is a workout app designed for High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), circuit training, and any workout that requires timed work and rest periods. Perfect for fitness enthusiasts who want structured, customizable workout routines.

  • ‚úÖ Custom Workout Routines - Create personalized interval workouts

üèãÔ∏èCreating Your First Routine

Step 1: Add a New Routine

  1. Open the app and go to the "Routines" tab
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-right corner
  3. Enter routine details:
    • Name: Give your routine a descriptive name (e.g., "Morning HIIT")
    • Sets: Number of work/rest cycles (1-99)
    • Prepare: Get-ready countdown before workout starts (0-60 seconds)
    • Work Duration: Active exercise time (minutes and seconds)
    • Rest Duration: Recovery time between sets (minutes and seconds)
    • Color: Choose a theme color for your routine

Step 2: Save Your Routine

  1. Review the summary showing total workout time
  2. Tap "Save" to create your routine
  3. Your routine appears in the main list

  2. Timer screen opens showing:

    • Current interval type (GET READY/WORK/REST)

üìäWorkout History

  4. Session details include:

  • Monthly overview: Scroll between months to see long-term progress

üîßTroubleshooting

Timer Issues

Timer not starting
  • Check if routine has valid work duration (> 0 seconds)
  • Ensure sets are configured (‚â• 1)
  • Try restarting the app
Timer stops unexpectedly
  • Check phone's Low Power Mode (can affect background audio)
  • Ensure app has microphone permission for audio session
  • Restart device if issue persists

Audio Issues

No voice announcements
  • Check "Voice Announcements" setting is enabled
  • Verify device volume is up
  • Test Siri voice in iOS Settings ‚Üí Siri & Search
  • Check if "Silent Mode" is enabled
No sound effects
  • Verify "Sound Effects" setting is enabled
  • Check device volume and silent switch
  • Test with headphones to isolate speaker issues

Purchase Issues

"Product not available" error
  • Check internet connection
  • Verify App Store account is signed in
  • Try again later (temporary server issues)

üí°Tips & Best Practices

Workout Preparation

  • Test your routine with shorter intervals first
  • Prepare your space before starting timer
  • Have water nearby for hydration breaks
  • Set phone to Do Not Disturb to avoid interruptions

Effective Interval Training

  • Warm up before high-intensity intervals
  • Focus on form over speed during work periods
  • Use rest periods for active recovery (light movement)
  • Cool down after completing workout

App Usage Tips

  • Create multiple routines for different workout types
  • Use descriptive names to quickly find routines
  • Color-code routines by intensity or body part
  • Check history regularly to track progress
  • Adjust intervals based on fitness improvements

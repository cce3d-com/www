What is HIIT & Tabata Training?

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) and Tabata are revolutionary workout methods that alternate between intense bursts of activity and brief recovery periods, delivering maximum results in minimum time.

üî•

HIIT Training

High-Intensity Interval Training involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by rest periods. Typically 15-45 seconds of work with 10-60 seconds of rest, proven to boost metabolism for up to 24 hours after your workout.

‚ö°

Tabata Protocol

A specific HIIT format: 20 seconds of ultra-intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated 8 times (4 minutes total). Originally developed by Dr. Izumi Tabata, proven to improve both aerobic and anaerobic fitness.

Science-Backed Results

Research shows HIIT can burn 25-30% more calories than other forms of exercise, improve cardiovascular health, increase insulin sensitivity, and preserve muscle mass while burning fat.
